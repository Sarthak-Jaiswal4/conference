“ Chemical Resilience & Sustainable Practices: Advanced materials in Water purification strategies for Desalination, Energy production & Enviromental conservation "

About the Conference

This International Conference, organized by the Departments of Chemical and Biochemical Engineering at Harcourt Butler Technical University (HBTU) in collaboration with the Indian Desalination Association (InDA), will focus on trends in desalination, water, energy, and the environment. Key topics include wastewater treatment, water resource management, purification methods, and waste management.

Experts will share insights on advanced material synthesis and its applications in tackling challenges in desalination, energy, and environmental sustainability. Technical sessions will highlight innovative technologies and strategies to address these pressing global issues effectively.

The event will foster collaboration among researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers. Through workshops and discussions, participants can exchange ideas on emerging solutions, contributing to advancements in sustainable water and energy management.
